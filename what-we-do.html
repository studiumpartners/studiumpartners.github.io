What We Do

Clear decisions on international expansion

International expansion presents a small number of high-impact decisions.

Most schools do not lack ambition. They lack clarity on where to focus, how to structure partnerships, and how to retain control over time.

We focus on those decisions.

Key Questions
  • Which markets should we prioritise, and why?
  • What level of demand exists, and how reliable is it?
  • What partner model is appropriate for our objectives?
  • How should governance be structured to protect standards?
  • What are the principal risks, and how can they be mitigated?
Outputs

Our work produces:

  • Market prioritisation based on demand, competition, and partner availability
  • Partner model definition, including commercial structure
  • Governance design covering academic control, brand, and oversight
  • Risk assessment across legal, operational, and reputational factors
  • A clear recommendation with alternative options
What We Do Not Do

We do not run long strategy programmes or generic market studies.

We focus on a small number of decisions and ensure they are made well.
