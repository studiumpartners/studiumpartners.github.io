About Studium Partners

Studium Partners is a specialist advisory firm focused on international expansion for UK independent schools.

We work with a small number of institutions each year, supporting senior leadership teams on high-stakes decisions.

Our approach is deliberately focused, analytical, and understated.

We prioritise clarity over volume, and decision quality over process.
