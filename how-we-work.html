How We Work

A focused, board-oriented process

Our work is delivered over 6-8 weeks.

We work closely with a small group of stakeholders, typically including the Head, Chair, and senior leadership team.

Phase Structure
  1. Framing
    Clarify objectives, constraints, and success criteria.
  2. Market assessment
    Evaluate target regions based on demand, competition, and feasibility.
  3. Partner model design
    Define appropriate structures and identify potential partner types.
  4. Governance and risk
    Develop a model that protects academic standards and brand integrity.
  5. Recommendation
    Deliver a clear, board-ready output with options and trade-offs.
Output

A concise, decision-ready document designed for board discussion.
