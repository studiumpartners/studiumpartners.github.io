Perspective

Market perspective

We work with a small number of schools and maintain an active view of the market.

The following themes are emerging:

Demand

Demand remains strong in specific regions, particularly GCC and parts of Asia. However, it is uneven and often overstated in early-stage discussions.

Partners

The availability of capital is not the constraint.

The quality and alignment of partners is.

Governance

Governance decisions made early tend to persist.

Weak structures are difficult to correct later.

Execution Risk

Operational complexity is often underestimated, particularly in maintaining educational standards across locations.
