What We Do

We advise school leadership teams on the markets, partners and governance for expansion.

The output is a prioritised set of markets, a clear partner model and a board-ready recommendation with explicit risks and trade-offs.

What We Are Seeing

Demand is strong in GCC and parts of Asia, but partner quality varies sharply.

The schools that avoid problems tend to specify governance early and understand operating complexity before committing.
