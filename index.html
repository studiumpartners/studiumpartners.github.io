Studium Partners

International expansion for UK schools

Many UK schools are reassessing international expansion as a way to protect and grow income.

We help leadership teams decide where to focus, how to choose partners, and how to protect governance and brand over time.

What We Do

We advise boards on the markets, partners and governance structures that make expansion durable.

The output is a prioritised set of markets, a clear partner model and a board-ready recommendation with explicit risks and trade-offs.

What We Are Seeing

Demand is strong in GCC and parts of Asia, but partner quality varies sharply.

The schools that avoid problems tend to specify governance early and understand operating complexity before committing.

Recent Work

Recent work has centred on market prioritisation, partner strategy and governance for international campuses.

This includes GCC vs APAC assessment, board advisory on control structures and partner pipeline development across multiple regions.

How We Work

We run a focused 6-8 week process, working directly with senior leadership.

The output is designed to support a clear board-level decision, not a prolonged strategy exercise.

If you are currently considering international expansion, we would be happy to share what we are seeing across the market.
